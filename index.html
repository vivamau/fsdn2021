Solutions' health: status analysis

Once a solution is reaching our team, it got a status that is going to be used to updated the progress of the analysis or of the support of the application during the FSDN journey. So the application can be in a under review status (27), or be ready for the Architectural Board review (none), or ready to go on production (3) or in production (3). An application then can be also discountinued (2) or (4) because a corporate solution is now available.

  • Under review
  • Waiting for CO
  • In production

By the way, the most critical status is when an application got a first analysis and a follow up is needed,but no one is reacting (37): this is a situation that needs to be sanitize with a better support of the management.

Solutions' quality: score analysis

In order to be able to put applications in a global context, we have introduced a score. Based on the different aspects, from documentation, to security, to hosting, to adeherence to WFP standards, the score trying to provide a good sense of what the quality of an application is, how far is the application to be acceptable and to do not be a threath for the organization. In this sense the score has to reach at least 60 points over 100 available.

  • Below 39
  • 40 - 59
  • Above 60

In this sense the score has to reach at least 60 points over 100 available. At the moment the number of applications that are over 60 is 7. The applications between 40 and 59 are 8, while the applications below 39 are 59.

The average score for the 74 applications is 32. In RBB is 17.5 (for 6 applications), in RBC is 25 (for 55 applications), in RBD is 38 (for 7 applications) and in RBJ is 68 (for 4 applications). RBN and RBP have only one application each.
